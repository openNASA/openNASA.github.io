dev/day

![Dev Day Header][]

Dev/day is a 7.5 hour-long intensive event that will be held on Tuesday July 9th, 2013, from 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM at NASA Johnson Space Center in the new 1958 collaborative space. The event is designed to allow a select group of 50 of the Agency’s brightest to conceive, design and implement compelling solutions to further NASA’s mission. This is a bold and innovative experiment in collectively inventing our future and we are excited that you have been chosen to participate in this inaugural event. We hope you will be able to join us!

The event is inspired by the International Space Apps Challenge, sponsored by the Office of Human Capital and hosted by the NASA Open Innovation Team.
