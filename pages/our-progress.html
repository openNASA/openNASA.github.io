Our Progress

The foundation of the Open Government movement at NASA is the Open Government Plan, which was released on April 7, 2010 as part of President Obama’s Open Government Directive. The principles of Open Government have been embedded in NASA operations for 50 plus years. The plan is our start in revisiting these concepts and creating a new level of openness and accountability in our policies, technology, and overall culture.

One of the most vital components of the plan is the over 150 milestones and goals set for organizations across the agency to implement Open Government-related programs and policies over the next five years.
